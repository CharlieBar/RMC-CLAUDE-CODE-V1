**H2: Frequently Asked Questions**

**Do I need an appointment?**
While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend calling ahead to ensure we can see you immediately. Same-day appointments are typically available.

**Will my auto insurance cover treatment?**
In most cases, yes. Illinois law requires auto insurance policies to include medical payment coverage. We'll verify your coverage and bill your insurance directly.

**How much will treatment cost me?**
Many patients pay nothing out-of-pocket when treatment is covered by auto insurance. We'll verify your coverage before starting treatment.

**Do I need a lawyer to get treatment?**
No, you don't need a lawyer to receive treatment. However, if you're working with an attorney, we'll coordinate with them to provide necessary documentation.

**How soon after my accident should I seek treatment?**
Immediately. Some injuries worsen over time, and getting prompt medical care is crucial for both your health and your insurance claim.

**What if I'm already seeing another doctor?**
We can provide a second opinion or complementary care. Bring your medical records to your first appointment.
